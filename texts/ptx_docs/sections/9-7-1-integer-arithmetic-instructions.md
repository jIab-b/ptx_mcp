### 9.7.1. Integer Arithmetic Instructions

Integer arithmetic instructions operate on the integer types in register and constant immediate forms. The integer arithmetic instructions are:

- `add`
- `sub`
- `mul`
- `mad`
- `clmad`
- `mul24`
- `mad24`
- `sad`
- `div`
- `rem`
- `abs`
- `neg`
- `min`
- `max`
- `popc`
- `clz`
- `bfind`
- `fns`
- `brev`
- `bfe`
- `bfi`
- `bmsk`
- `szext`
- `dp4a`
- `dp2a`
